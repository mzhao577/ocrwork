Scanned Documents
•Screen readers or other assistive technology are 
unable to convert images scanned into text.
•Scanned documents, especially poor quality scans or handwriting, can be difficult to read for everyone.
•Scanned documents should be avoided as much 
as possible.
•If the document can be found in the library, a reference to the library item can be provided.
•Benefits of having digital copy
•Can be searched
•Can copy and paste text
