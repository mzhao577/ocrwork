Mobile payments—led by Alipay and WeChat Pay—have become ubiquitous, with cash usage declining sharply. Key innovations include: • QR-code payment systems • Super-app ecosystems • Smart logistics and warehouse automation • Livestream commerce China’s fintech sector has also pioneered digital lending, wealth management, and insurance technologies, supported by vast user data and advanced risk-scoring algorithms. 7. Space Technology and Aerospace China’s space program has made remarkable strides, transitioning from satellite launches to deep-space exploration. Achievements include: • The Chang’e lunar missions, including the first soft landing on the far side of the moon • The Tianwen-1 Mars mission • The construction of the Tiangong space station • Rapid development of commercial launch companies These accomplishments reflect China’s growing capabilities in propulsion, materials science, and aerospace engineering. 8.
