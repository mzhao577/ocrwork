Option 1: No Source Document? Try Export Options
When no source document is available, 
recommend opening the PDF in Acrobat, and exporting to a Word or PowerPoint file. 
To convert a PDF to a Word document, select 
File > Export To> Microsoft Word > Word 
Document .
Next step is applying Styles Formatting . See 
the next slide “Direct vs Styles Formatting.”
