UDC Mission

The UDC supports the campus community in

their efforts to make it possible for individuals to
learn, communicate, and share via information and
communication technology. One way we do this is

by assisting the campus community to ensure their
information and communication technology is
interoperable, usable and accessible, so that individual
learning and processing styles and/or physical
characteristics are not barriers to access.

Campus Responsibility
Everyone has a part in creating accessible and usable
information. The responsibility of creating and

maintaining accessible content falls to the entire
campus community.
