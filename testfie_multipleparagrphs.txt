Nothing about the situation felt predictable anymore.

by the gate,
hoping for
good news.

across the quiet street,
painting the pavement
with silver ripples.

The research team gathered the last set of observations before concluding the study. Although the results were not entirely expected, they revealed patterns that could guide future experiments. The group planned to publish a preliminary report within the next month.

in the empty hall,
fading quickly
into silence.

As the conference wrapped up, participants exchanged final thoughts and contact information. Many expressed excitement about continuing the conversations that had begun during the sessions. The organizers promised to release recordings and materials within the week.
